Hello! I am Anthony Giusti

Audio Enigneer and Self Taught Coder

About me:

During my time earning my Bachelor of Arts from Columbia College Chicago I primarily worked on student films doing audio design and production. I already had been digitally creating music for years so I found myself greatly enjoying the creativity of audio production.

Outside of school, I did my first freelance jobs in the events industry in 2011. A few years later after finishing my degree I transitioned to full time employment in the events industry. I enjoy event production since it allows you to take elements of audio, video, camera, projection and other technologies and combine them in interesting and creative ways.

I have been coding on and off for a few years as one of my hobbies. I enjoy the problem solving aspect as well as creativity of it which I find similarly gratifying to my experience working with sound design or music composition. I have primarily worked with HTML, CSS, SASS, JavaScript, React and other front end tools. Although I have also dabbled in other technologies such as C#, SQL and Object Oriented Programming.
