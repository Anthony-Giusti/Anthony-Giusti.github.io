About me:

During my time earning my degree at Columbia College Chicago I primarily worked on student films during audio design and production. I already had been digitally creating music for years so I enjoy the creativity of audio production. I believe sound aspect of films is very important but often overlooked piece of the art form a good sound design can definitely make or break a film.

Outside of school, I did my first freelance job in the events industry in 2011. A few years later after finishing my Audio degree I transitioned to full time employment in the events industry. I enjoy event production since it allows you take to elements of audio, video, camera, projection, computers and other technologies and combine them in interesting and creative ways.
